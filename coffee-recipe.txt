Ingredients:
 - 1 coffee tea spoon
 - 2 sugar tea spoons
 - 1 cup of water

Directions:
 1. Put the cup of water to boil
 
 2. In a filtering device, put the coffee tea spoon (in the filter) and add the sugar spoons (into the bottom cup).
 
 3. When water is boiling, pour it into the filter and let it throught the bottom cup, mix the sugar and serve it in a mug.
